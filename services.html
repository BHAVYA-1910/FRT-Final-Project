Farm Livestock

Farm livestock refers to the animals that are raised on farms for various purposes such as meat, milk, eggs, wool, and other by-products. Livestock on farms may include cows, pigs, sheep, goats, chickens, ducks, turkeys, and other domesticated animals.

Garden Tillage

Garden tillage refers to the process of preparing garden soil for planting by mechanically break up and turning over the soil. Tiller which may be operated or with the help of a machine. The purpose of garden tillage is to create a aerated soil bed that is free of weeds , making it easier for plant roots to grow .

Fresh Fruits

Fresh fruits are unprocessed and uncooked fruits that are picked at the peak of ripeness, which means that they are full of flavor, vitamins, and minerals. Fresh fruits are typically grown in orchards, fields, or on trees, and they come in a wide variety of shapes, sizes, and colors.

Vegatables

Vegetables are edible plants that are an essential part of a healthy diet. They are grown in gardens, fields, and farms, and come in a wide range of colors, sizes, and shapes. Vegetables are rich in vitamins, minerals, fiber, and other nutrients that are important for maintaining good health and preventing chronic diseases.

Awesome Wheats

Wheat is one of the most widely cultivated and consumed cereal grains in the world, with a rich history dating back thousands of years. This versatile grain is a staple food in many countries and is used to make a wide range of products, including bread, pasta, pastries, and breakfast cereals.

Agro Machinery

Agro machinery refers to a variety of tools, equipment, and machines that are used in farming and agriculture to help farmers carry out tasks more efficiently and effectively. These machines can range from simple hand tools such as hoes and shovels to more complex machinery like tractors, combines, and irrigation systems.

The Crop

A crop is a plant that is grown by farmers on agricultural land to produce food, fiber, or fuel. Crops can be categorized into two main groups: food crops and cash crops. Food crops are grown for human consumption and include grains such as rice, wheat, and corn, as well as vegetables and fruits.

Fresh Fruits

Fresh fruits come in a wide range of colors, shapes, and sizes, and can be eaten raw or cooked. Some of the most popular fresh fruits include apples, bananas, oranges, strawberries, grapes, and pineapples.

Agro Machinery

Agro machinery includes a wide range of tools and equipment, from simple hand tools like shovels and hoes, to more complex machines like tractors, plows, harvesters, and irrigation systems. These machines are used for a variety of tasks, including soil preparation, planting, cultivation, harvesting, and irrigation.
